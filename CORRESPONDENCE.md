Correspondence

Definition

Correspondence is the process of examining whether a testimony consistently corresponds to its object, definition, evidence and reasoning.

对应，是检验证词是否与其对象、定义、证据和推理保持一致的过程。

MoWen does not begin with judgment.

MoWen begins with correspondence.

莫问不始于裁决。

莫问始于对应。

---

Why Correspondence

A testimony cannot be honestly evaluated without correspondence.

Without correspondence, conclusions cannot be reliably supported.

证词若没有对应，就无法得到诚实检验。

没有对应，结论就无法得到可靠支持。

---

Objects of Correspondence

Every testimony may correspond to:

- Object
- Definition
- Evidence
- Existing Knowledge
- Reasoning

证词可以对应：

- 对象
- 定义
- 证据
- 已有知识
- 推理

---

Honest Runtime

Correspondence is the core of MoWen Runtime.

Every runtime verifies correspondence before generating conclusions.

对应，是莫问诚实运行的核心。

每一次运行，都必须先完成对应，再生成结论。

---

Responsibility

Correspondence does not create testimony.

Correspondence does not create evidence.

Correspondence does not create conclusions.

Correspondence only examines whether they correspond.

对应不会创造证词。

对应不会创造证据。

对应不会创造结论。

对应只检验它们是否保持对应。

---

Principle

Without correspondence, testimony cannot become a trustworthy conclusion.

Without correspondence, responsibility cannot be honestly returned.

没有对应，证词无法成为值得信赖的结论。

没有对应，责任无法被诚实地归还。
