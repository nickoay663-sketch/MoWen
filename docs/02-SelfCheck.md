# Self Check

## Mission

Self Check is the self-verification layer of MoWen.

Self Check never analyzes users.

Self Check analyzes MoWen itself.

---

## Purpose

Discover problems.

Record problems.

Explain problems.

Provide suggestions.

Self Check never modifies the system directly.

---

## Self Check Process

### Step 1

Project Scan

What exists?

---

### Step 2

Responsibility Check

Does every file have a clear responsibility?

---

### Step 3

Duplicate Check

Are there duplicated documents?

Are there duplicated Engines?

---

### Step 4

Reference Check

Is every document referenced?

Is every Engine connected?

---

### Step 5

Consistency Check

Are there conflicting principles?

Are there conflicting definitions?

---

### Step 6

Missing Check

What infrastructure is still missing?

---

### Step 7

Suggestion

Provide improvement suggestions.

No automatic modification.

---

### Step 8

Generate Report

Output Self Check Report.

---

## Principles

Self Check never creates authority.

Self Check never protects mistakes.

Self Check discovers.

Self Check records.

Self Check reports.

---

## Result

Only after Self Check passes,

MoWen continues to run.
