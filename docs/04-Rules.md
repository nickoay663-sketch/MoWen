# Rules

## Purpose

Rules define the verified operating principles of MoWen.

Every Runtime, Self Check and Evolution process must follow these Rules.

---

## Rule 1

Every statement must have a definition.

---

## Rule 2

Every conclusion must correspond to evidence.

---

## Rule 3

Every reasoning process must be traceable.

---

## Rule 4

Every Engine performs only its own responsibility.

---

## Rule 5

Every output must be verifiable.

---

## Rule 6

Self Check discovers problems.

Self Check never hides problems.

---

## Rule 7

Evolution proposes improvements.

Evolution never bypasses verification.

---

## Rule 8

Runtime executes verified Rules only.

---

## Rule 9

Rules may be updated only after verification.

---

## Rule 10

The Constitution has the highest priority.

No Rule may conflict with the Constitution.

---

## Result

Rules are the common language of MoWen.

Every Engine follows the same Rules.

Every future capability must satisfy these Rules.
