# Evolution

## Mission

Evolution is the evolution layer of MoWen.

Evolution improves MoWen through honest reasoning.

Evolution never changes the Constitution directly.

---

## Purpose

Discover limitations.

Analyze causes.

Reason about improvements.

Generate new proposals.

Submit proposals for verification.

---

## Evolution Process

### Step 1

Receive Self Check Report.

---

### Step 2

Analyze the problem.

What caused it?

---

### Step 3

Reason possible solutions.

Can the existing principles solve it?

Is a new principle required?

---

### Step 4

Generate Proposal.

Every proposal must include:

Problem

Reason

Evidence

Expected Result

---

### Step 5

Verification.

Every proposal must pass verification before adoption.

---

### Step 6

Update Rules.

Only verified proposals become official Rules.

---

## Principles

Evolution never modifies Runtime directly.

Evolution never bypasses Self Check.

Evolution never creates authority.

Evolution improves only through verification.

---

## Result

MoWen evolves continuously.

Every improvement must remain honest.

Every evolution must be verifiable.
