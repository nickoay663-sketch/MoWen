MoWen Symbol

Concept Symbol

The MoWen symbol is a concept symbol.

It is not merely a logo.

It visually expresses the philosophy of MoWen.

---

Symbol Structure

The symbol is based on the complete Spanish question mark.

The question mark remains unchanged.

        ¿
        M
        W
        ?

      MoWen

The letters M and W are placed vertically inside the question mark.

- M is above.
- W is below.
- The letters are smaller than the question mark.
- The question mark remains the primary visual element.

The word MoWen appears below the symbol as the official brand name.

---

Letter Design

The letters M and W may be slightly transformed into smooth curved forms.

The transformation must preserve the recognizable shapes of the letters.

The curved design represents:

- continuous runtime;
- continuous correspondence;
- continuous verification;
- continuous correction.

The symbol expresses an honest runtime that never stops improving.

---

Meaning

The outer Spanish question mark represents inquiry.

The inner MW represents MoWen.

The curved letters represent continuous honest runtime.

The complete symbol represents:

A question containing MoWen.

MoWen does not begin with an answer.

MoWen begins with a question.

MoWen continuously verifies, corresponds and corrects.

Visual Principles

- Keep the complete Spanish question mark unchanged.
- Keep M above W.
- Keep the letters smaller than the question mark.
- Preserve the recognizable shapes of M and W.
- Curved forms may be used to express continuous runtime.
- Maintain a clean and minimal design.
- The symbol should communicate honesty before conclusion.
