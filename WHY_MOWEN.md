# Why MoWen

## The Internet connected the world.

But it did not connect trustworthy people.

---

Information became faster.

Trust became weaker.

Knowledge increased.

Responsibility disappeared.

Opinions spread.

Evidence became blurred.

---

MoWen was not created to produce answers.

MoWen was created to execute honesty.

It starts from testimony.

It ends with responsibility.

---

MoWen does not decide for people.

MoWen gives every testimony an honest execution.

Every conclusion must be able to return to its evidence.

Every statement must be able to bear its own responsibility.

---

The future of civilization is not more information.

The future of civilization is trustworthy language.

That is why MoWen exists.
