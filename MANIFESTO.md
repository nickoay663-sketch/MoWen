# MoWen Manifesto

互联网连接了世界。

莫问连接了信任。

诚实，是文明开始的地方。

---

MoWen is an Honest Runtime.

MoWen begins with testimony.

MoWen does not replace human judgment.

MoWen does not define truth.

MoWen restores responsibility.

MoWen asks questions before conclusions.

Trust begins where honesty begins.

Civilization begins where responsibility returns.
