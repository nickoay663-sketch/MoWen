# Reasoning

## Definition

Reasoning is the process of deriving conclusions from verified premises.

Reasoning does not create premises.

Reasoning depends on verified correspondence.

---

推理，是根据已经验证的前提得出结论的过程。

推理不会创造前提。

推理依赖已经验证的对应关系。

---

## Runtime Meaning

Reasoning begins only after Correspondence has been completed.

Without verified correspondence, Runtime cannot perform reliable reasoning.

---

只有完成 Correspondence 后，Reasoning 才开始。

没有经过验证的对应，Runtime 不能进行可靠推理。

---

## Requirements

Reasoning requires:

- Testimony
- Definition
- Evidence
- Correspondence

If any required element is missing,

Reasoning cannot be completed.

---

推理需要：

- 证词
- 定义
- 证据
- 对应

任何一项缺失，

推理不能完成。

---

## Responsibility

Reasoning produces conclusions.

The responsibility for the testimony remains with the testimony provider.

MoWen is responsible only for the runtime process.

---

推理负责产生结论。

证词的责任仍属于证词提供者。

莫问只对运行过程负责。
