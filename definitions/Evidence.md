# Evidence

## Definition

Evidence is any verifiable information that can support or contradict testimony.

Evidence does not produce conclusions.

Evidence only exists to be examined.

---

## Runtime Meaning

Evidence is collected after testimony has been recognized.

Evidence is the foundation of correspondence.

---

## Requirements

Evidence must be:

- Verifiable
- Traceable
- Reproducible

Evidence that cannot be verified is not Runtime evidence.

---

## Correspondence

Runtime compares testimony with available evidence.

Correspondence is determined by verification, not belief.

---

## Responsibility

Evidence does not bear responsibility.

The use of evidence bears responsibility.
